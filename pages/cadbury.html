Logo Sketches

DESIGN RATIONALE

Project Objective

This is Cadbury chocolate milk flavoured with orange. The objective goal was to pick any flavour to pair with Cadbury chocolate, and then I had to create a logo for the flavour I chosed.

Target Audience

12-25 years old who enjoy drinking orange juice without worrying about consuming too much sugar.

Concept

The logo I want to incorporate a fresh orange juice with the straw straight to orange. It feels more organic and fresh. I added leaf onto the straw and added a strong punch of green for additional contrast, so the logo doesn't feel a little bit warm feeling in terms of colour palette.

Challenges

The design concept here is minimal; I had to keep the original colour and font of Cudbary and some other elements that I don't fully control.

Design Process

For the font, I have to use the original font of Cadbury branding, but the colour I had to add my own choice, but I have to limit the use of colour to remain the feel of the original design
