Logo Refinements

DESIGN RATIONALE

Project Objective

This project was to create a logo identity of a coffee shop located in Niagara Falls. This coffee shop is selling not only premade coffee but signature bulk coffee.

Target Audience

Most tourists in Niagara falls are family and couples. 20-30 years old celebrating holidays and anniversaries. Families want to explore Niagara falls while drinking coffee. This small local coffee shop is a unique brand identity that suits Niagara falls.

Concept

It is very unique made specify here in Niagara Falls. The logo is symmetrical with two meanings, waves from the falls and once combine side by side logo resembling the shape of a heart. The heart represents romantic love and affection. Many tourists pick Niagara fall destination for their honeymoon. This logo is suitable for its identity. The colours are yellow and gray ironically; these two colours are the colour of the year according to Pantone.

Challenges

The challenge for this project was that the client doesn't want to include a coffee cup/mugs and coffee beans/leaf part of the logo. Instead of He wanted that speaks to the native Niagara falls brand.

Outcome

The brand identity was unique, and it represents the theme of the falls

Design Process

The first phase was to create many variations of a logo that is unique of its kind and has multiple meanings. Once identify the right logo, I had to explore possible outcomes. The last step was to clean and make it simplified as possible.

Logo Meaning

Symmetry design. it has two meaning a wave that represents the falls and heart once combine side by side
